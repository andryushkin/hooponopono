🌊 Ho'oponopono (pronounced "ho-oh-POH-noh-POH-noh", and often searched as Hooponopono, Ho oponopono, or Ho-oponopono) is an ancient Hawaiian ritual of forgiveness, healing, and deep reconciliation. The exact ho'oponopono meaning translates to "to make right," "to correct an error," or "to rectify."
